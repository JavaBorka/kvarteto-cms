The project is co-financed by the Governments of Czechia, Hungary, Poland and Slovakia through Visegrad Grants from International Visegrad Fund. The mission of the fund is to advance ideas for sustainable regional cooperation in Central Europe.

Project ID number:

22410383

Project title:

Commemorating 25 years of V4 Quartet magazine, Visegrad Seal festival, Memorial publication, Film

Project short description:

Our project aims to highlight the importance of constant communication of V4 partner countries. We would like to become a good example of long-lasting cooperation to a wide public. Visegrad Seal Festival will be a Gala event commemorating 25 years of bringing topics to our audiences. We plan to prepare and launch a memorial publication - 25 years of Quartet and a documentary film of our journey.
