The project is co-financed by the Governments of Czechia, Hungary, Poland and Slovakia through Visegrad Grants from International Visegrad Fund. The mission of the fund is to advance ideas for sustainable regional cooperation in Central Europe. Read more about project here.

About us

Four TV Studios from public service televisions of each of the Visegrad Four countries prepare a common TV magazine monthly.

All the four partners have always been equal cooperators. We began to prepare a common television magazine called QUARTET in 2000. Since then we have never ceased to produce it, although the directors general and political situations changed. RTVS - TV Studio Košice, ČT - TV Studio Brno and Ostrava, MTVA - Duna TV Szeged, TVP - TVP3 Rzeszow.

Dramaturg, scriptwriter and director working for more than 3 decades in the Czech Television. She is a member of the working group of the Czech Government Council for National Minorities. Author of a number of documentaries and series such as Babylon, Neighbours, Folk walks, Folk magazine and others.
